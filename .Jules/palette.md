## 2024-05-24 - Accessible hover-only UI elements
**Learning:** Icon-only action buttons that rely on `opacity-0 group-hover:opacity-100` are completely inaccessible to keyboard users because they cannot be seen when focused.
**Action:** Always combine `group-hover:opacity-100` with `focus-within:opacity-100` on the container or `focus-visible:opacity-100` on the button itself. Ensure standard keyboard accessibility with `focus-visible:ring-2 focus-visible:outline-none` and provide `aria-label`s for screen readers.
