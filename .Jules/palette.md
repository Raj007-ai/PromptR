## 2024-05-24 - Accessible Hover Buttons
**Learning:** Icon-only action buttons that rely purely on hover states (`opacity-0 group-hover:opacity-100`) are inaccessible to keyboard users because they remain hidden during tab navigation.
**Action:** Always combine `group-hover:opacity-100` with `focus-within:opacity-100` on the container or `focus-visible:opacity-100` on the button itself, and ensure `aria-label` and `focus-visible:ring-2` are added for proper focus states.
