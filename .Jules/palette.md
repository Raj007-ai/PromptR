## 2025-02-20 - Keyboard Accessibility on Hover-Only UI Elements
**Learning:** Icon-only action buttons that rely on `opacity-0 group-hover:opacity-100` are entirely inaccessible to keyboard users because they remain invisible during tabbing.
**Action:** To ensure accessibility, always combine `group-hover:opacity-100` with `focus-within:opacity-100` on the container or `focus-visible:opacity-100` on the button itself. Alongside this, apply `focus-visible:ring-2` and `outline-none` to provide a clear visual focus ring, and ensure `aria-label` is present for screen readers.
